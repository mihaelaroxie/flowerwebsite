About Us

... our journey begins here

It all started in a small town near Seoul.
Our grandma had made us flower crowns ever since we were kids.
As we grew up and she got older, we wanted to do something she used to love.
She wanted to be a florist since she was a little girl, but unfortunately she couldn't.
So we wanted to turn her dream into reality, hence thet name of our shop.
